## Marketplace platform

This marketplace platform allows users to easily search for the products or services they need. With access to a list of marketplaces, users can choose between different options and choose the provider that best suits their needs. Also, the ability to use the map to view the exact location of the markets ensures users to quickly and accurately continue their search and find the nearest provider. In addition, the presence of a chat section allows users to talk with others about their experiences with markets and share their opinions and suggestions. This application tries to improve the process of buying, selling, and renting products and services for users and provide them with a simple and satisfying experience.
